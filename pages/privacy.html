Privacy Policy

Effective June 20, 2026 Error Dev Group
Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Error Dev Group collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.
Section 1

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of our services. This may include:

Type Examples When collected
Account info Username, email address, password (hashed) When you sign up
Transaction data Purchase history, items bought, amounts paid When you make a purchase
Usage data Pages visited, features used, timestamps While using the site
Communications Support messages, Discord DMs sent to us When you contact us
Device info Browser type, IP address, operating system Automatically on visit

We do not collect payment card numbers directly. Payment processing is handled by third-party providers.

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process and fulfill your purchases
  • Communicate with you about orders, support, and updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • Improve and maintain our website and services
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

Section 3

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Some data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage, such as your shop listings and cart. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

While we take security seriously, no system is completely foolproof. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

Section 4

Cookies & Local Storage

Our website may use cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Store cart and listing data locally on your device
  • Maintain session state for a smoother experience

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings, though this may log you out and reset your preferences.

Section 5

Third-Party Services

We may share limited information with third-party services that help us operate. These include:

  • Payment processors — to complete transactions (they receive billing information as necessary)
  • Roblox — when delivering Robux or account-related purchases
  • Discord — when you contact us via our Discord server for support

These third parties have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We do not control their data practices.

Section 6

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law or to resolve disputes.

Transaction records may be retained for longer periods for legal and accounting purposes.

Section 7

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via Discord. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Section 8

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

Users between 13 and 17 should have parental consent before using our services, as outlined in our Terms of Service.

Section 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Section 10

Contact Us

Discord
